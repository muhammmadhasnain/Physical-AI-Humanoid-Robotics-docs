# Module 3.2 – Humanoid Robot Development

## 4.1 Kinematics & Dynamics

### Topics

- FK/IK
- Multi-body dynamics
- Center of Mass
- Zero Moment Point (ZMP)

### Outcomes

- Compute stable humanoid poses & movements.

### Topics

- Gait generation
- Balance control
- Uneven terrain walking
- Fall detection

### Outcomes

- Simulate stable, controlled humanoid walking.

## 4.3 Manipulation & Grasping

### Topics

- Arm trajectory planning
- Multi-finger hand control
- Object grasping
- Isaac grasping SDK

### Outcomes

- Implement realistic grasping & manipulation.

## 4.4 Natural Human–Robot Interaction (HRI)

### Topics

- Voice & gesture commands
- Pose estimation
- Human-safe behavior modeling

### Outcomes

- Enable natural communication between humans & humanoid robots.
